      <h1>Directory Structure</h1>
      <h2>Before Running</h2>
      The Generic Transform tool expects the following structure:
      <ul>
         <li>base dir</li>
         <li>base dir\data</li>
            <ul><li>contains your data file(s)</li></ul>
         <li>base dir\settings</li>
            <ul><li>contains your settings file(s)</li></ul>
         <li>base dir\template</li>
            <ul><li>contains your template file(s)</li></ul>
      </ul>
      <h2>Example</h2>
      The following is an example of a proper directory structure:<br>
      <img border="0" src="directoryBefore.png"\>

      <h2>After Running</h2>
      Furthermore, the tool automatically creates two additional directories:
      <ul>
         <li>base dir\log</li>
            <ul><li>contains the logs generated by the program</li></ul>
         <li>base dir\out</li>
            <ul><li>contains the output generated by the program</li></ul>
      </ul>
      <h2>Example</h2>
      After running the tool your base directory will look something like this:<br>
